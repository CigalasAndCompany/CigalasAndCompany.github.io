Games

Gutierrez in Mondongo's Kingdom

This is our first game, a run and gun 2d Pixel-Art game, heavily focused on boss fights. We took clear inspiration from games like Cuphead, as we want to give players a considerable amount of difficulty whilst having a satisfiying and fluid combat system. The game takes part in the imaginary kingdom of Mondongo's, a villanous king based in today's Murcia, Spain. It takes a fun and comical approach to medieval settings and enemies. Also, try to find all the easter eggs in the game, it sure is a challenge on its own!
